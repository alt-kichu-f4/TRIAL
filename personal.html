Books

  • Harry Potter Series – J K Rowling

    • A fantasy series about a young boy who discovers he is a wizard and attends a magical school where learning spells somehow involves constant danger.   • Alchemist – Paulo Coelho

    • A philosophical novel about a shepherd named Santiago who goes on a journey to find treasure and accidentally finds himself instead. The story focuses on dreams, destiny, and listening to one’s heart, repeatedly reminding you that the universe is allegedly rooting for you. Simple, symbolic, and motivational, it’s the kind of book that feels gentle on the surface but keeps poking you about your life choices long after you finish it.

  • Oru Sankeerthanam Pole – Perumpadavam Shreedharan

    • A Malayalam novel that presents the life of Russian writer Fyodor Dostoevsky through the perspective of his wife, Anna. The book explores love, sacrifice, faith, suffering, and artistic obsession with quiet emotional depth rather than drama. Written in a reflective, almost musical tone, it shows how greatness is often built on pain, patience, and unwavering support from someone who chooses to stay.

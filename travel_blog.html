Jadayuppara (Jatayu Earth’s Center)

Location: Kollam, Kerala

Jadayuppara, also known asss Jatayu Earth’s Center, is a popular tourist destination located in the Kollam district of Kerala. The place is famous for the massive sculpture of Jatayu, inspired by the Ramayana, and offers scenic views of the surrounding hills and forests.

The visit was memorable due to the peaceful atmosphere and the panoramic view from the top. The location combines mythology, nature, and modern tourism, making it a good place to visit with friends and family.
